You’re set.

One click turns the YouTube video you’re watching into a Gemini summary.

  1. Click the 🧩 extensions (puzzle) icon at the top-right of Chrome.
  2. Find YouTube Summary with Gemini: Open Source One-Click Summarizer and click its pin icon so it lives on your toolbar.
  3. Open any YouTube video — the toolbar icon shows a green dot when it’s ready.
  4. Click it. Gemini opens and starts summarizing.
Want a different prompt? Right-click the icon → Options. The video’s title and link are added automatically.
